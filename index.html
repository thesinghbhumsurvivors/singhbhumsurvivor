About Us

Singhbhum Survivor is a grassroots NGO working in West Singhbhum, Jharkhand. Grounded in our NSS roots, we work with rural communities to dismantle barriers to learning and spread awareness about the importance of education.

Mission: To bridge the gap in rural education by mobilizing the energy of students and the expertise of professionals, empowering the next generation with literacy, skills, and foundational knowledge needed to thrive.

Our Work

Education

We organize learning classes, distribute books and study materials, and mentor rural students.

Awareness Campaigns

We sensitize parents and communities about the transformative power of education.

Clothes Distribution

We conduct cloth distribution drives to support dignity and basic needs.
